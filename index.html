About me

May 2018

Working as QA Executive at Ipsos Interactive Services

Eager to test the waters of the job market and see how far my programming skills can take me, I decided to work as a Quality Assurance Executive before going to university.

Here I learned the hard lessons about deadlines, managing workloads giving estimates and, above all, teamwork

In the meantime, I became more and more engrossed in JavaScript, and decided that Web Development is my field of choice.

Thus, I began to study the server side of it, with Node.js, Express and Typescript as foundation.

October 2019

Studying at "Titu Maiorescu" University

It soon became evident that much more good can be accomplished by working on the client side as well. Moreover, after the first tutorial, things just clicked.

I've left my position at Ipsos Interactive Services to focus on studying Web Development more diligently, but to also prepare for the University Degree I have chosen: Computer Science, obviously.

For now

The future is promising

Nowadays, I spend my time studying arduously, both for the school curriculum and my development skills, always on the lookout for a new challenge!
